The Best Movies According to Yours Truley

The Top 3 of All Time

Blazing Saddles

A weastern comedy by Mel Brooks about the first sheriff of color in a small town called Rock Ridge.
This was just a really funny film. It's a bit dark and vulger at times, but it sends a pretty good message.
Also further supporting how great of an actor Gene Wilder was.

Little Evil

Another comedy, this time be Eli Craig. It's about a new stepdad who starts believeing that his
5 year-old son may in fact be the antichrist.

Rush Hour

Another lovely comedy, this one by Brett Ratner. This is about a decetive from Hong Kong and a detective
from the LAPD reluctingly working together to rescue the kidnapped daughter of a Chinese Diplomat.
Jackie Chan and Chris Tucker play Inspector Lee and Detective James Carter respectivley.
